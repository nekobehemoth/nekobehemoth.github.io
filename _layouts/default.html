Everyone's Japanese

Mn

This stunning site will help you start learning Japanese. The site is built on the basis of the popular textbook "Minna No Nihongo", which has won a leading position among other textbooks. The material of the lessons is supplemented with exercises from other textbooks, which will allow you to better understand the grammar of the lesson. All content in Russian.

KanjiDB

Kj

This site will help you find unknown kanji or words. Almost all words have a tonification and an example of pronunciation that will allow you to improve your own pronunciation. Also there is the opportunity to test your knowledge of kanji and help memorize them.

NHK World

NW

NHK World is a multi-lingual site dedicated to Japan. The site is the link of Japan and the rest of the world, it provides reliable and timely information about the state of affairs in Japan in various countries of the world, also allows you to get acquainted with the culture and lifestyle of the Japanese. Also on the site there are lessons of the Japanese language aimed at the development of oral speech.
